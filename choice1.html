The First Avengers

First Avengers Marvel Comics, The Avengers #1,September. 1963

The very first time the Avengers made an appearance was in 1963.The original team conisisted of Ant-Man, The Hulk, Iron man, Thor, and the Wasp. The reason was The Avengers was created was because DC Comics made the Justice league, which is a team of superheroes. The fans lovedd it so Marvel decided to do the same and make a team of superheroes. This team of The Avengers only lasted two issues but it is the orginial team.The Avengers in today's movies are actually an alternate version of the Avengers.

Dark Avenger

Dark Avengers Marvel Comics, Dark Avengers #1, March, 2009

The DarK Avengers are exactly what they sound like. The evil villian Norman Osborn had taken over S.H.I.E.L.D. He then wanted to make an Avengers to team aid him in his missions. The biggest difference in this team is that none of the team were actually superheroes. They had a fake, captain America, Thor, Hawkeye, and Captain Marvel. Not only did they have fake Avengers the team also had Bullseye, Moonstone and Venom which were all villians. They also had Wolverine. The Dark Avenger is a comic based on what would happen if basically villains took other the team, instead of actaul good guys, who resigned after Osborn was made the leader.
